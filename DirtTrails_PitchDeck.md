# Dirt Trails Pitch Deck

---

## 1. The Problem

- Tour businesses in Africa struggle with low digital visibility and limited access to tech.
- Tourists face scams, unreliable operators, and lack of trusted information.

---

## 2. Our Solution

- **Dirt Trails**: An authentic digital marketplace for tours, activities, and experiences.
- Only verified and vetted operators are listed—no scams, no fakes.
- Seamless, tech-driven discovery and booking for tourists and locals.

---

## 3. Uniqueness & Differentiation

- **Operator Verification**: Every business is vetted for authenticity and quality.
- **Conservation Tracker**: Every booking supports local conservation, tracked transparently.
- **Local Focus**: Built for Africa, by Africans, with deep local partnerships.
- **All-in-One**: Hotels, tours, transport, events, and more—one platform, one login.

---

## 4. MVP Features

- Search & filter by city, category, and price.
- Real-time availability and instant booking.
- Operator profiles with reviews and verification badges.
- Secure payments and booking confirmations.
- Conservation impact tracker for every booking.

---

## 5. Business Model

- **Commission**: 10-20% on every booking.
- **Premium Listings**: Operators pay for featured placement.
- **Conservation Partnerships**: Brands sponsor conservation efforts, visible to users.
- **Data Insights**: Sell anonymized market insights to partners.

---

## 6. Traction & Opportunity

- 100+ operators onboarded in pilot phase.
- Partnerships with local conservation NGOs.
- Growing demand for authentic, safe travel experiences in Africa.

---

## 7. Conservation Tracker: Responsibility in Action

- Every booking funds a local conservation project.
- Users see their impact in real time—trees planted, wildlife protected, communities supported.
- Transparent reporting and stories from the field.

---

## 8. The Team

- Local founders with deep travel, tech, and conservation experience.
- Advisors from tourism, tech, and impact sectors.
